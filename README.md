# 🐶 A Dog's Adventure

A small third-person exploration game developed as part of the **Realtime 3D Engines** course at Heilbronn University.

In this game, you play as a stranded dog exploring a lonely tropical island. Search for hidden bones, dig them up, and find your way through the island while enjoying a relaxed exploration experience.

---

## 🎮 Features

- Third-person character controller
- Open tropical island to explore
- Hidden collectible bones
- Digging interaction
- Animations for walking, running, jumping and digging
- Cinemachine camera
- Simple UI and sound effects
